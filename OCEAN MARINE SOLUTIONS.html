About Us

Leading the way in Nigeria's marine industry with innovation and expertise

Ocean Marine Solutions Nigeria

Founded in 2008, Ocean Marine Solutions has established itself as a premier provider of marine and offshore services in Nigeria. With our headquarters in Lagos and operations across major Nigerian ports, we serve the oil & gas, shipping, and maritime sectors.

Our team of experienced mariners, engineers, and maritime professionals are committed to delivering safe, efficient, and compliant solutions tailored to the unique challenges of operating in Nigerian waters.

We are fully licensed and registered with all relevant Nigerian maritime authorities including NIMASA, NPA, and comply with international standards and best practices.

Our mission is to enhance the safety, efficiency, and sustainability of marine operations in Nigeria through innovative solutions and unparalleled expertise.
